Our Pastors

Pastor Kofi Dartey

Apostolic Overseer

Pastor Kofi Dartey is the founder and General overseer of Campus Rush Global, and the Apostolic Overseer of the Tent Church Worldwide. With over 10 years of leadership experience, and itinerant ministry, his passion remains building the local church. He loves Jesus, people, and is devoted to building life-giving churches that are led by the Holy Spirit. Mixing his background as a Registered Social worker, JUNO nominated musician and Doctor of theology holder, he provides charismatic and visionary leadership to the Tent.

Our Pastors

Dr. Ralph Dartey II

Prophetic Overseer

Dr. Ralph is the Founding Pastor of Bridge YTH, the youth expression of Transforming Life Centre Church in Ottawa. Serving as the Assistant Pastor of Campus Rush, since 2016, he now serves as the Prophetic Oversight of the Tent Church.

Dr. Dartey holds a Ph.D. in Ministry and Theology, he also earned a Master of Arts in Public Policy, and a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science, with a minor in French. Beyond his academic accomplishments, he operates in the prophetic office and is recognized as a revivalist preacher with a deep passion to see people cultivate an intimate relationship with the Lord.
