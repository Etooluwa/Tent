We believe the outdoors is
the original, uncrafted
sanctuary.

Tent Church is a gathering of people who find peace, connection, and divine
presence under the open sky. We trade stained glass for towering pines, and
wooden pews for camp chairs. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or just looking for
a breath of fresh air, there's a place for you by the fire.

Our Pastor

Pastor Kofi Dartey

For over a decade, we've devoted our lives to full-time ministry, serving, teaching, and helping people engage deeply with God's Word. Our journey has taken us from youth pastoring to itinerant evangelistic ministry, to founding platforms committed to helping Christians learn the Bible.

But through it all, our heart for the local church has remained at the core. It's where we found our own lives transformed, where generational curses were broken, and where we discovered our purpose. Now, we believe it's time to create that same transformative space for others here.
