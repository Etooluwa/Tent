Transforming Lives • Raising Leaders on University Campuses

Although Tent is the city church, there is also a campus church that was started on university campuses in 2014 at Carleton University.

The campus church is called Campus Rush and this was the ministry that evolved over time into Tent Church to reach a wider demographic than just university and college students.

Every city you find a Campus Rush, you'll find a Tent location. They share a common DNA of transforming lives and raising leaders.
