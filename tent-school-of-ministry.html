Equipping Leaders • Shaping Future

Tent School of Ministry (TSM), founded by Dr. Kofi Dartey (through Tent Church Global) is a Bible and Ministry Training Institution dedicated to equipping biblically grounded, spiritually mature, and practically skilled leaders.

As the ministry’s influence has grown beyond local congregations, there has been a growing need for structured, doctrinally sound, and leadership-focused theological education accessible to both emerging and established ministry leaders.

The program will feature interactive discussion forums, practical assignments, and in-person intensives, ensuring both rigorous academic engagement and real-world application.
